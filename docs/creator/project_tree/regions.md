title: "Regions"
sidebar_position: 1
---

Regions are the maps in your game which define the world, dungeons and towns.

You use the **geometry tools** to create geometry in the regions. Regions can be viewed using a **2D** or various **3D** cameras.

## Creating a Region

You can create a **region** by clicking the **+** button at the bottom of the **project tree** and selecting **Region**.

## Deleting a Region

With the region selected, click the **-** button at the bottom of the **project tree**.
